How to play

  • Drag through adjacent tiles to form English words. Diagonals are allowed.
  • Each tile can be used at most once in a word.
  • Green means a new valid word, yellow means already found, and red means invalid.
  • Progress is a weighted percentage: more common words count slightly more.
  • The heatmap shows how many remaining valid words start from each tile. Darker tiles have more remaining starts.
  • Find 4 valid words within 10 seconds for a special effect.
