🌱 What this app is for

This is not meant to be a perfect self-improvement dashboard. The goal is simpler: spend more of your day actually living your life instead of automatically opening your phone.

  • Run one productive timer whenever you are genuinely focused.
  • Press the ugly social button whenever you open social media or unlock your phone mindlessly. The friction is intentional.
  • The rings are meant to be emotional, not precise. The productive ring filling up should feel satisfying. The social ring filling up should feel slightly uncomfortable.
  • Clean-focus trophies reward uninterrupted focus sessions with no social media breaks.
  • The habits are intentionally lightweight. They are small behaviors that tend to make the rest of the day easier.
  • Do not obsess over optimization. The point is awareness and momentum, not perfection.
